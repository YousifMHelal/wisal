## **Project Operational & Technical Requirements:** 

Call Center system shall be scalable according to HHC expansion needs. The system shall include but is not limited to the following requirements: 

- High Availability for 24/7 including support plan for all levels. 

- Comply with National Cybersecurity Agency (NCA). 

- Call location Identification (optional). 

- Dashboard integration with NMR (Live) using Open API. 

- Work force management 

- Quality management 

- Capable of being hosted by Private or Public Cloud or other types of Data centers. 

- Operation Workflow. 

- Call recordings (recording and playback simultaneously) and analytics. 

- Centralized Technology / Decentralized Operations Capabilities. 

- Scalable according to future growth provision. 

- Mobile App.

- Streamline communication (Inbound /Outbound) with unified inbox (Voice, Email, SMS, WhatsApp…etc). 

- Support SIP trunk, E1 Integration ….etc. 

- Capable to Integrate video call when needed by API port . 

- Call routing and escalation. 

- System performance analysis and reporting. 

- Monitor quality profile. 

- Automatic Call Distribution (ACD). 

- Call Whispering. 

- Callback management. 

- Agent Status & performance dashboard.
